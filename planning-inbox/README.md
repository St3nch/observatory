# Planning Inbox

Status: active working area  
Purpose: rough planning notes, triage docs, prompts, and non-authoritative exploration for The Observatory.

---

## Purpose

`planning-inbox/` is where early ideas live before they are promoted into authority docs, contracts, research outputs, roadmap milestones, or decisions.

This folder is useful, but it is not project law.

---

## What Belongs Here

- rough working notes;
- repo-first triage notes;
- prompts and prompt outputs worth preserving;
- early research agendas;
- non-authoritative strategy explorations;
- unresolved questions;
- parking-lot material not yet promoted.

---

## What Does Not Belong Here

- accepted boundary law;
- schema contracts;
- implementation code;
- migrations;
- authoritative decisions;
- customer records;
- provider secrets;
- paid capture logs;
- final research outputs once promoted.

---

## Reading Order

1. `observatory-working-notes.md` — broad early working notes and idea capture.
2. `repo-first-research-triage.md` — repo-grounded triage of research questions already partly answered by ancestor/adjacent repos.
3. Any future agenda/prompt docs — read only after the two files above unless a milestone says otherwise.

## Notes For LLMs

Do not treat this folder as authority.

If a planning-inbox idea conflicts with `00-project-overview.md`, `01-harvest-register.md`, `02-boundaries.md`, or `ROADMAP.md`, stop and reconcile.

Ideas in this folder must be promoted through roadmap/owner review before they can become contracts, schema, implementation, or project law.
