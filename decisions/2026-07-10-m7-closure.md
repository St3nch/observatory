## Decision

M7 is closed as a contract-drafting milestone, and M8 is activated as the next planning milestone.


## Scope

This decision applies to:

- closing M7 as the active roadmap milestone;
- activating M8 as the active roadmap milestone;
- treating the thirteen M7 contract documents as the draft set for M8 hammer planning;
- carrying unresolved owner rulings forward as fail-closed constraints.

This decision does not apply to:

- accepting all contract drafts as final law;
- authorizing schema design;
- authorizing migrations;
- authorizing implementation;
- admitting any provider;
- authorizing paid pulls;
- creating API/MCP tools;
- creating dashboards;
- storing customer data;
- storing strategy, recommendations, or accepted conclusions.
